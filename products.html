Unions

Unions are the five factions lead by the Five Foretellers. The Five Foretellers are Keyblade Masters, apprenticed to the same Master and each bestowed with a copy of the Book of Prophecy. Each Foreteller has an associated animal, and hides their identity behind a mask representing that animal.
