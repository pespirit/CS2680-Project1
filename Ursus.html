Ursus

Ursus is one of the five Unions the player can join in Unchained X. The animal representing the Union is a bear.
The Ursus Foreteller is a man who wears brown robes with a cream hood and a bear mask hiding his face. His Keyblade is brown and gray, with a rock motif.
