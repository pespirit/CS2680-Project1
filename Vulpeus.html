Vulpeus

Vulpeus is one of the five Unions the player can join in Unchained X. The animal representing the Union is a fox.
The Vulpeus Foreteller is a girl, who wears white and brown robes, with a pink hood and a fox mask hiding her face. Her Keyblade is pastel, with a swirling air motif.
