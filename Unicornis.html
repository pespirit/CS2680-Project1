Unicornis

Unicornis is one of the five Unions the player can join in Unchained X. The animal representing the Union is a Unicorn.
The Unicornis Foreteller is a man who wears white and blue robes with a white hood and a unicorn mask hiding his face. His Keyblade is white and black, with a moon-like motif.
